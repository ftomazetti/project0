Frederico Tomazetti

I was born on the second last day of the year 1970 right on the border of Brazil and Argentina. Since the childhood I enjoyed music, two-wheeled vehicles and toys that required logic and reasoning to be played with.

I learned computer coding at the age of 18, back there when computers were made of green screens, magnetic tapes and floppy disks. In these 30 years of experience I have lived so many things in the IT area that it would be necessary many gigabytes on a drive to put everything away.

In 1997 I moved to Minas Gerais, another state in Brazil where I was supposed to work for only one year. 23 years later and I`m still in Minas, I really liked it, I got married here and it became home to me.

Nowadays I work as a developer at the QA area, test automation, bots and internet programming.

Software development, music and motorcycling are my three passions since my adolescence and they are part of the story you will read here.

Belo Horizonte MG Brazil 2020
